The mats are produced from closed-cell PE-foam and have the following features:

  • Soft and comfortable
  • Great heat insulation
  • The material does not absorb water
  • Elastic band with a buckle for carrying on your waist
  • Lightweight
  • Can be easily attached to your backpack
  • Many possible use applications

The cushion can be attached to the waist with a strap and this way it always stays clean and dry. One does not need to carry it around the camping site and it is always there to sit on it: just pull it down from the waist. The mat can be produced with a logo or a slogan. The mat is also a convenient gear for spectaculars of various mass outdoor events, e.g. pop concerts and sport competitions.

It can work as orginal a marketing tool, business gift, merchandising material or a group attribute of a club or company. This product will be taking care of your customers for years.
