Outdoor sitting mats

Protect youself at any surface!

Our product is a lightweight cushion (PE-foam mat) used for sitting outdoor in harsh environments, like wet, cold and rough surface. The cushion comes with a printed logo or name of a company, event, club etc.

This is an ideal product for various outdoor activities, for example: camping, grilling, hiking, skiing, fishing, hunting, birdwatching, berry picking, school excursions, music festivals, sport events etc. Thanks to the elastic stripe, you do not need to carry the mat around; it is always with you when you need to sit down on a wet, cold or harsh surface. It does not get wet when it’s raining and it won’t be blown away with wind. And you do not need to squeeze it into your backpack: just attach it outside of your backpack with the stripe.

The mats are new and unique product at the Finnish market. Such mats with a logo of your company or club make a great promotional tool and a business gift. Please read more about the use cases and ask questions through the contact form.
